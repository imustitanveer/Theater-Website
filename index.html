The Monolith Theater

Welcome to the Monolith!

With it's 100 years of history, The Monolith Theater stems as a marvel of the city, showcasing the best of talent, that is offered by the region. Each Day, the theater showcases a live action play with it's cast of diverse actors, and their exceptional talent at putting on the mantel of the marvelous cahracters our writers come up with. The Monolith has historically had the price of $1 per show, which has been kept throughout it's history and we plan on keeping it that way. Initially, a place only for the rich to enjoy their time at, today, The Monolith welcomes everyone into its gargantuan premises, hosting plays for all to blow the week's steam off with.
